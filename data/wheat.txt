Wheat is one of the major crops grown in Punjab.
Wheat requires cool weather during growth.
Nitrogen fertilizer improves wheat yield.
Farmers should irrigate wheat fields regularly.
Leaf rust is a common disease in wheat crops.
Proper seed selection increases productivity.
Punjab Agricultural University recommends timely irrigation.
Organic manure improves soil fertility.
Overwatering may damage wheat roots.
Balanced fertilizer use is important for healthy crops.
